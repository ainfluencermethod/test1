Day 1
01
Pick your niche

The niche is the leverage point. It shapes who the audience is, what style of content works, how easy monetization becomes, and whether the algorithm can classify the page fast.

What strong niches look like

Popular angles

Gamer girl, cosplay, girl-next-door, athletic, luxury lifestyle, fetish-adjacent, alt, nerdy, couples.

Specificity wins

Blend physical type + vibe + interest. Example: athletic gamer girl. That makes targeting easy.

Use this filter before committing

  • Does the niche already have pages with serious traction?
  • Can you generate consistent images in this lane?
  • Does the audience have obvious spending behavior?
Pro tip: generic characters get generic results. A clear, fetishized, or highly legible character concept almost always performs better.
Day 1 · Foundation
Day 2
02
Build your DNA file

Your AI character is an asset. The DNA file is the operating system behind it.

1. Niche statement

Write one sentence explaining who she is and who she’s for.

2. Prompt anchor

Define age, visual traits, body type, styling, vibe, and signature details you’ll reuse in every prompt.

3. Reference image

Generate 10–15 images and choose the single best one as the visual anchor.

Build a swipe file too

  • Save top-performing reels and TikToks in your niche
  • Note hook, format, angle, caption style, and comments
  • Track posts with high saves and high retention
Consistency matters more than novelty early on. Your audience should recognize the character in one second.
Day 2 · Build Your AI Model
Day 3
03
Launch sequence + account warm-up

New accounts get tested. If the first signals are weak, distribution dies fast. Warm-up is how you avoid 300-view jail.

  • Create the new page from an existing account center if possible
  • Engage 3x per day in your exact niche for 15 minutes each
  • Post your first 5–7 pieces and review outliers immediately
  • Double down on the winning format and kill weak angles fast

Signals that matter

Watch time rising while skip rate drops is one of the strongest “keep pushing this” signals you can get from the algorithm.

Do not “wait and see.” Train the algorithm actively through your behavior and your content.
Day 3 · Account Warm-Up
Day 4
04
Find and recreate what already works

The fastest path to virality is not inventing formats. It’s finding proven concepts and adapting them with your character.

The Goldmine Method

  • Search your niche and sort by recent winners or most liked
  • Save 10 examples with strong hooks and obvious emotional triggers
  • Identify the core reason each one worked: desire, curiosity, humor, shock, relatability
  • Recreate the concept using your model’s DNA and stronger execution

What to copy

Hook structure, pacing, emotional trigger, camera energy, caption intent.

What to make your own

Your character, your visual identity, your exact scenes, your narrative layer.

Day 4 · Goldmine Content
Day 5 + 6
Automation + algorithm mastery

Weekly production system

  • Monday: pick 7 proven concepts
  • Tuesday: generate 20–35 image variations
  • Wednesday: edit, caption, schedule
  • Thursday–Sunday: engage, analyze, and identify outliers

Metrics to engineer

Watch time

Front-load the strongest visual instantly.

Skip rate

Weak opening = dead post.

Saves

High-value content gets revisited.

Shares

Emotion drives free distribution.

Comments

Clear opinions create conversation.

Profile visits

Curiosity converts to follows.

Batching is a power move. Stop asking “what do I post today?” and start building a machine with inventory.

Day 7
07
Get paid with Fanvue

Followers are not the business. Monetization is the business.

  • Create the Fanvue account using consistent branding
  • Start around $9.99/month
  • Upload the first 5 monetizable pieces of content
  • Put the link in bio everywhere
  • Use stories and teasers to convert free attention into paid traffic

The math

At $9.99/month, roughly 150 paying subscribers gets you to about $1,500/month. You don’t need millions of followers. You need targeted traffic and a conversion path from day one.

Set up monetization before you feel “ready.” Every follower you gain before that is revenue you delay.
