# AI Universa — YouTube Trailer Script
### Dolžina: 75–85 sekund | Tempo: HITER | Jezik: Slovenščina (tikanje)

---

## 🎬 PRE-PRODUCTION NOTES

- **Format:** 16:9, 1080p minimum (4K preferred)
- **Editing style:** Quick cuts (0.5–1.5s per shot), motion graphics, kinetic typography
- **Color grade:** Dark base, purple/blue accent lighting, high contrast
- **Music:** EDM/trap build → drop at 0:15 → driving beat through middle → final crescendo at CTA
- **Suggested track mood:** Cinematic tension → energetic drop → triumphant finale

---

## SCRIPT

---

### ⚡ 0:00–0:05 — HOOK (5s)

**🎵 Music:** Deep bass pulse. Tension. Heartbeat-like rhythm.

**VISUAL:** Black screen. Numbers SLAM onto screen one by one with impact sound effects. Each number appears as bold kinetic typography with particle effects.

> **TEXT OVERLAY (slam on):** 200.000.000+ ogledov
> **TEXT OVERLAY (slam on):** 155.000+ sledilcev
> **TEXT OVERLAY (slam on):** €41.127+ zaslužka učencev

**🎬 B-roll:** Ultra-fast montage (0.3s cuts): YouTube analytics dashboard → follower counter spinning → screenshots of student earnings → Pici filming content → phone notifications exploding.

**⏱ Pacing:** Each stat hits like a punch. No breathing room. BOOM. BOOM. BOOM.

---

### 🔥 0:05–0:15 — PROBLEM (10s)

**🎵 Music:** Sharp cut to silence → single piano note → ominous low drone builds.

**VISUAL:** Pici walks toward camera in dark studio, single spotlight. Confident but serious.

> **PICI (direct to camera, serious tone):**
> "AI spreminja vse. Službe. Biznis. Način kako zaslužiš denar."
>
> *(beat — slight pause, leans in)*
>
> "In večina ljudi? Bo ostala zadaj."

**🎬 B-roll (intercut with Pici):**
- AI-generated visuals of automation replacing jobs (0.5s)
- News headlines about AI disruption — Slovenian sources (0.5s)
- Person scrolling phone looking overwhelmed (0.5s)

**TEXT OVERLAY:** "AI SPREMINJA VSE." (glitch effect, centered)

**⏱ Pacing:** Slower than hook — let the weight land. The contrast creates tension.

---

### 🚀 0:15–0:30 — TEASE / REVEAL (15s)

**🎵 Music:** BUILD. Synth layers stack. Rising pitch. Anticipation.

> **PICI (energy shifts — now excited, slight smile):**
> "Zato sem pripravil nekaj posebnega."

**VISUAL:** Camera pushes in on Pici. Quick cuts accelerate.

> **PICI:**
> "3 dni. V živo. Brezplačno."

**🎵 Music:** DROP. Full beat kicks in. Energy explodes.

**VISUAL:** AI Universa logo SLAMS onto screen with shockwave effect. Purple/blue particles radiate outward. Event branding reveal — cinematic, polished.

> **TEXT OVERLAY (animated, bold):**
> **AI UNIVERSA**
> 15.–17. APRIL 2026
> BREZPLAČNA DELAVNICA V ŽIVO

**🎬 B-roll:** Quick flash of Pici teaching live → engaged audience on Zoom/chat → screen recordings of AI tools in action.

> **PICI (voiceover, high energy):**
> "AI Universa. Tri dni, kjer ti pokažem točno kako danes zaslužiš z AI-jem."

**⏱ Pacing:** Rapid acceleration into the drop. The reveal should feel like an EVENT.

---

### 📚 0:30–0:50 — WHAT YOU'LL LEARN — Day 1/2/3 (20s)

**🎵 Music:** Driving, confident beat. Steady momentum.

**VISUAL:** Split into 3 segments, each ~6 seconds. Day number appears as large typography before each.

---

**DAY 1 (6s):**

**TEXT OVERLAY:** "DAN 1" (large, glitch-in)

> **PICI (voiceover, rapid-fire):**
> "Dan 1 — pokažem ti natančen sistem, s katerim moji učenci že zaslužijo s pomočjo AI-ja."

**🎬 B-roll:** Screen recording of AI workflow → money notification sounds → laptop with earnings dashboard.

---

**DAY 2 (6s):**

**TEXT OVERLAY:** "DAN 2" (large, glitch-in)

> **PICI (voiceover):**
> "Dan 2 — skupaj zgradiva tvoj prvi AI posel. V živo. Korak za korakom."

**🎬 B-roll:** Hands on keyboard building something → Pici live-demoing on screen → split screen of tool + results.

---

**DAY 3 (6s):**

**TEXT OVERLAY:** "DAN 3" (large, glitch-in)

> **PICI (voiceover):**
> "Dan 3 — avtomatizacija. Da ti AI dela, medtem ko ti spiš."

**🎬 B-roll:** Automation flows running → phone notifications of sales → person relaxing while screen shows activity.

**⏱ Pacing:** Rat-tat-tat. Each day is a bullet point. No lingering.

---

### 🏆 0:50–1:05 — SOCIAL PROOF (15s)

**🎵 Music:** Beat continues, add inspirational layer (strings or pads).

**VISUAL:** Student results flash on screen — screenshot style with names, like a rapid-fire testimonial reel. Each gets 2–2.5 seconds.

> **PICI (voiceover, proud tone):**
> "To ni teorija. Poglej rezultate."

| Student | Visual | Overlay Text |
|---------|--------|-------------|
| **Luka** | Screenshot of earnings | "Luka — €2.840 v 30 dneh" |
| **Ana** | Screenshot of client message | "Ana — prvi klient v 5 dneh" |
| **Matej** | Screenshot of dashboard | "Matej — dal odpoved, dela s polnim časom z AI" |
| **Nina** | Screenshot of payment | "Nina — €1.200 poleg redne službe" |
| **Gregor** | Screenshot of results | "Gregor — 0 na €3.500/mesec" |
| **Tina** | Screenshot of message | "Tina — 'Najboljša odločitev mojega življenja'" |

**🎬 Transition:** Each result SLAMS in with a whoosh sound effect, slight zoom, then cuts to next.

**⏱ Pacing:** Machine-gun style. The sheer VOLUME of results is the message.

---

### 🎁 1:05–1:20 — BONUSES (15s)

**🎵 Music:** Energy peak. Add rising synth. Build to climax.

> **PICI (to camera, leaning in, excited):**
> "Pa to ni vse."

**VISUAL:** Pici in studio. Camera slowly pushes in.

> **PICI:**
> "Vsi prijavljeni dobijo brezplačen prompt paket — moji najboljši prompti, ki jih sam uporabljam vsak dan."

**TEXT OVERLAY (animated list, items pop in):**
✅ Brezplačen AI Prompt Paket

> **PICI (voice rises, big energy):**
> "In med vsemi prijavljenimi žrebamo — AVTO..."

**🎬 Visual:** Car image SLAMS on screen with impact effect.

> "...3 MacBooke..."

**🎬 Visual:** MacBook images cascade in.

> "...in iPhone."

**🎬 Visual:** iPhone drops in. All prizes visible on screen together.

**TEXT OVERLAY (final prize spread):**
🚗 AVTO | 💻 3x MacBook | 📱 iPhone

**⏱ Pacing:** Build, build, BUILD. Each prize is a mic-drop moment.

---

### 🎯 1:20–1:30 — CTA / END CARD (10s)

**🎵 Music:** Final drop → resolves to powerful, clean ending. Last note sustains.

**VISUAL:** Pici, close-up, dead serious into camera. Single light.

> **PICI (direct, commanding, no BS):**
> "15. april. 19:00. Prijavi se. Brezplačno."

*(points down / gestures to link below)*

> "Link je spodaj. Vidimo se v živo."

**VISUAL (END CARD — holds 4 seconds):**

```
│     15.–17. APRIL 2026 · 19:00      │

│    ▶ PRIJAVI SE BREZPLAČNO ◀         │
│                                      │

```

**TEXT OVERLAY:** aiuniversa.si (large, centered, glowing)
**SUBSCRIBE ANIMATION:** YouTube subscribe button + bell animation (bottom right)

**🎵 Music:** Clean resolve. Silence hits at exact end.
